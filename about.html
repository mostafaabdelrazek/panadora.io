About Panadora

We, Panadora, came into existence in the year of 2015 are a prominent manufacturer, exporter and supplier of a wide array of seeds, Herbs, dehydrated products, Every year we sort and pack more than 1000 Ton of seeds and Herbs for our clients EU, USA and Far east . We collaborate with our trusted / contracted farms located in Bahria oasis 500 km away far from the old contaminated area in delta. In which the use of pesticides is prohibited by law.

For the quality, Offered products are processed by taking optimum quality basic components as per the set industry norms and standards at our well-equipped processing unit.

We are offering these products in various packaging options to suit the ever-changing demands of our clients in effective manner.

To achieve the set organizational goals and objectives, our professionals are working with full dedication and support. Apart from this, we are supported by our strong distribution network; with the help of this network, we have been able to deliver the offered products within the promised frame of time safely.

We have always been dedicated to the complete satisfaction of our clients and value long-term business relations with them. After years of experience, we have developed a growing international spirit. Our expanding clients around the world highly appreciate our quality products grown on the fertile soil of Egypt.

Good Agricultural practices (GAP) are not just about produce. It is also about people. So it is our interest to protect one of the most important resources of farms, its people. Herbs cultivation and collection industry is consuming children as a labor, As Pandora is SEEDX /SMETA Certified, we wants to be certain children are not involved in the process and that farmers receive fair compensation for their work.
